Beaker automatically creates a private "System Drive" for storing your private information. It contains your saved hyperdrives, bookmarks, and more.

You can find the drive by opening the browser menu and selecting "My Private Drive."

Beaker assigns your system drive the hyper://private/ domain name. You cannot share content using the system drive, as each user has their own hyper://private/ address. (This is a good thing because your system drive contains private information!)
